Benefits are added incentives given by companies and employers to coworkers that take many different forms. Examples include medical care coverage and disability pay.

⚠️ LIABILITY WARNING

When your benefits are infringed upon or taken advantage of by your employer, they may be liable to stand trial for benefits fraud, workers compensation fraud, or other charges for denying your rights.

VOCATIONAL REHAB

If you are unable to perform your usual duties, you may need to be re-trained for a new trade. Also covers physical therapy to obtain pre-existing physical strength.

EMPLOYER CONDUCT & PENALTIES

If your employer's conduct imposes on your physical well-being or restricts you from gaining your physical strength (e.g., neglecting inquiry into the accident), he will be penalized and should be reported to deter such conduct in future situations.
