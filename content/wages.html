Wage loss benefits are designed to replace lost wages while the worker is temporarily unable to work. This ensures financial stability during recovery.

IMMEDIATE RELIEF

CONTINUATION OF PAYMENT (COP)

Pay that is the equivalent of an employee’s regular salary for up to 45 calendar days of wage loss.

⚠️ COP is not considered compensation and is subject to income tax & retirement deductions.
TIMELINE RULES
Standard Rule: Payable after a 3-day waiting period following the expiration of the 45 days.
The Exception: No waiting period is required when permanent disability exists, or when the disability exceeds 14 days.

LONG-TERM IMPACT

Where injury-related loss of earning capacity persists after the schedule award ends, compensation may be continued for loss of earning capacity.
