Vocational Rehabilitation

Call for Help: (888) 595-4LAW

Vocational rehabilitation facilitates an individual who has lost his job in returning to a status of employment or obtaining training for a new profession. It assists the individual to prepare physically, emotionally, and psychologically to cope with life changes.

JOB MATCHING

Matches specific skills and interests to find the right role for the individual.

JOB PLACEMENT

Finds a job (usually a partnership) and instructs the individual in effective job-seeking skills.

VOCATIONAL COUNSELING

Assesses skills, abilities, and interests to create a comprehensive employment plan.

CUSTOM TRAINING

Trains specific technical or soft skills that a future job or new trade may require.

ASSISTIVE TECHNOLOGY

Special devices or accommodations that assist an individual to better perform duties at home or work.

INDEPENDENT LIVING

Assists in managing tasks more efficiently or identifying resources around the home.

A TAILORED APPROACH

Services are tailored to each individual. We assist those with mental or physical disabilities that make it hard to find or keep a job.

KEY REQUIREMENT

Individuals seeking vocational rehabilitation services must have a drive to work. We build the assessment based on your skills, abilities, and goals.
